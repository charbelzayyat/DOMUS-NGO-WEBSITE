About DOMUS

Bringing hope, dignity, and compassion to those in need since 2002.

Our Story

Founded in 2002, DOMUS started as a humanitarian initiative dedicated to supporting elderly people and families in need.

Throughout the years, DOMUS expanded its mission through food distribution, clothing donations, educational programs, social activities, and spiritual support.
